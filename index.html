Our Mission

Founded in 2024, SupplyOpt is a decision science consulting firm built by professionals from operations research, manufacturing, supply chain, and data science backgrounds, with years of hands-on industry experience. Our core team comes from IIT Bombay's Industrial Engineering and Operations Research department, with years of industrial experience across diverse sectors. We understand real industrial problems — not just theory.

At SupplyOpt, our mission is simple: help industries perform better using data-driven decisions. We help organizations:

  • Replace guesswork with reliable, data-backed decisions
  • Improve performance using modern analytical tools
  • Reduce dependency on manual planning and firefighting

Our solutions are designed so teams can see the impact clearly — in cost, time, and profit. We work closely with manufacturing plants, warehouses, and transportation firms to improve operational efficiency, profitability, and long-term resilience.

Strategic Simulation

We build realistic simulations of plants and warehouses to test decisions before implementation. This helps validate strategies, reduce risk, and avoid costly trial-and-error on the shop floor.

Mathematical Optimization

Our optimization experts design solutions tailored to your business goals — such as reducing cost, improving schedules, and better resource utilization — across production, warehousing, and transportation.

AI / ML Solutions

We use AI and machine learning to build intelligent models for predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and operational intelligence, helping prevent breakdowns and improve reliability.

Demand Forecasting

We provide accurate and robust demand forecasts so manufacturers can plan production confidently and respond effectively to future demand changes.

Inventory Optimization

Our models determine reliable inventory levels for each product, helping you avoid stock-outs, reduce excess inventory, and never lose upcoming orders.

Production Scheduling

We design optimized production schedules that help you get maximum output from existing resources, reduce delays, and improve on-time delivery.
